Assault rifles:

STG44

Selective-fire pistol, cal. 7.92x33mm Kurzpatrone tutaj

German WW2 selective-fire pistol STG44 - cal.7.92x33 mm 'Kurzpatrone'

Available on our shooting range!

The StG 44 was the first successful and widely produced design to use a new shorter cartridge, which permitted controllable automatic fire from a weapon more compact than a battle rifle, coupled with the recognition that most aimed rifle fire in combat situations did not exceed a few hundred metres.

AK74

Automatic rifle, cal. 5.45mm tutaj

Kalashnikov automatic rifle model 1974 - cal 5.45x39 mm

Available on our shooting range!

Developed in the early 1970s by Russian designer Mikhail Kalashnikov as the replacement for the earlier AKM (itself a refined version of the AK-47). It uses a smaller 5.45×39mm cartridge, replacing the 7.62×39mm chambering of earlier Kalashnikov-pattern weapons.



Pistols:

Glock 19

Semi-automatic pistol Glock19 - cal.9x19 mm tutaj

Austrian semi-automatic pistol Glock19 - cal.9x19 mm

Available on our shooting range!

The Glock pistol is a series of polymer-framed, short recoil-operated, locked-breech semi-automatic pistols designed and produced by Austrian Glock Ges.m.b.H.. It entered Austrian military and police service by 1982 after it was the top performer in reliability and safety tests.

Italian semi-automatic pistol Beretta 92F - cal.9x19 mm 'Parabellum'

Available on our shooting range!

The Beretta 92 is a series of semi-automatic pistols designed and manufactured by Beretta of Italy. The model 92 was designed in 1972 and production of many variants in different calibers continues today.

Walther P99

German semi-automatic pistol Walther P99 - cal.9x19 mm 'Parabellum'

Available on our shooting range!

The Walther P99 is a semi-automatic pistol developed by the German company Carl Walther GmbH Sportwaffen of Ulm for law enforcement, security forces and the civilian shooting market as a replacement for the Walther P5 and the P88.



Rewolwers:

Smith & Wesson Model 500

Large-caliber revolver produced by Smith & Wesson, firing the cal.50' tutaj

five-shot, double/single action large-caliber revolver produced by Smith & Wesson, firing the .500 S&W Magnum cartridge, a .50 caliber bullet.

Available on our shooting range!

The Model 500 can fire a bullet weighing 350 gr (22.7 g; 0.8 oz) at 1,975 feet per second (602 m/s) generating a muzzle energy of over 3,030 foot-pounds force (4.1 kJ) and a momentum of 13.7 Newton seconds. Commercial loadings are available in bullet weights ranging from 275 gr to 700 gr. The Model 500 is capable of firing the shorter .500 S&W Special cartridge.
